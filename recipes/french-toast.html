French Toast

Description

French toast is an easy and quick meal made by dipping bread in a lightly seasoned egg mixture and cooking it until golden and crispy. Itâ€™s soft on the inside, slightly crunchy on the outside, and perfect for breakfast or a light meal when you want something warm and filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili powder or green chili
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Oil or butter

Steps

  1. Crack the egg into a bowl.
  2. Add milk, salt, pepper, and chili.
  3. Beat everything well with a fork.
  4. Add chopped onion and mix again.
  5. Heat a pan on medium heat and add oil or butter.
  6. Dip one slice of bread into the egg mixture (coat both sides).
  7. Place it on the pan and cook until golden.
  8. Flip and cook the other side.
  9. Repeat with the second slice and serve hot.
