Egg Sandwich

Description

Egg Toast is a simple and comforting snack where a perfectly cooked egg is placed on warm, toasted bread. Itâ€™s quick to make, filling, and can be enjoyed plain or with a little seasoning, making it a perfect breakfast or anytime bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 slice cheese
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion (optional but nice)
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  3. Beat gently with a fork.
  4. Heat a pan and add butter or oil.
  5. Add onion (if using) and cook until soft.
  6. Pour in the eggs and scramble slowly.
  7. Place cheese on top and let it melt.
  8. Toast the bread lightly.
  9. Put the egg mixture between the bread and press gently.
