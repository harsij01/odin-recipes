Pancake

Description

Pancakes are a quick and easy breakfast made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, sugar, and a little baking powder. The ingredients are mixed until smooth, then poured onto a hot pan and cooked until golden on both sides. Theyâ€™re soft, fluffy, and perfect served warm with toppings like syrup, fruit, or butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Put the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a bowl. Mix them well so the dry ingredients are evenly combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, crack the egg and add the milk, melted butter or oil, and vanilla essence if using. Whisk everything together until the mixture is smooth.In a separate bowl, crack the egg and add the milk, melted butter or oil, and vanilla essence if using. Whisk everything together until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Slowly pour the wet mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Gently mix until just combined, making sure not to overmix even if there are a few small lumps.
  4. Let the pancake batter rest for about 5 to 10 minutes. This resting time helps the baking powder work and makes the pancakes softer and fluffier.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or frying pan on medium heat and lightly grease it with a little butter or oil. Make sure the pan is warm but not too hot.
  6. Pour about one quarter cup of batter into the pan for each pancake. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges look set, then gently flip it over.
  7. Cook the other side for about one minute or until golden brown. Remove the pancake from the pan and repeat with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve the pancakes warm with honey, syrup, butter, fruits, or any topping you like. Enjoy your homemade pancakes.
